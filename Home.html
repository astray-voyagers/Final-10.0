Why?

The reason to create this website is in order to deliver a more streamline information regarding Video games to Professional Gamers or Newbies alike. This website will present the history of video games and how games were like in the past compared to nowadays. Apart from the interesting history of video games, this website will also present fascinating works that many big or small game studios did up till now. Other than that, this website will also include the particulars of video game genres and a few examples to provide insights to how the game genres differ from one another.
